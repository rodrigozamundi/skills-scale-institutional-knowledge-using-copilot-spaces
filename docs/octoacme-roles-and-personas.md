# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Core Project Management Roles

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Product Managers on prioritization and roadmap alignment
- Works with Developers and QA to track execution and identify blockers
- Partners with Scrum Masters to facilitate agile ceremonies and remove impediments
- Escalates risks and dependencies to Sponsors and Stakeholders
- Receives customer feedback from Customer Success Leads to inform timeline adjustments

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interactions
- Aligns with Project Managers on timeline and resource constraints
- Collaborates with UX Designers on feature design and user experience
- Works with Data Analysts to validate hypotheses and measure feature impact
- Receives feedback from Customer Success Leads on user adoption and satisfaction
- Consults with Developers on technical feasibility and estimates

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interactions
- Partners with QA/Testing specialists to ensure quality standards
- Collaborates with UX Designers on feature implementation and usability
- Works with Release Managers on deployment readiness and rollback procedures
- Coordinates with Scrum Masters on sprint planning and impediment removal
- Receives guidance from Data Analysts on instrumentation and metrics collection

---

## Quality Assurance & Testing

### Role Summary
QA/Testing specialists validate quality and acceptance criteria, ensuring features meet customer needs and maintain system reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Execute test plans and acceptance criteria validation
- Conduct exploratory testing and edge case analysis
- Report and track defects through resolution
- Collaborate on test strategy and automation approaches
- Perform regression testing before releases

### Goals
- Ensure features meet acceptance criteria before release
- Maintain high quality standards and reduce production incidents
- Build team confidence in delivery

### Typical Communication
- Testing updates during daily standups
- Defect reports and test results in project board
- Pre-release readiness assessments

### Interactions
- Works closely with Developers on test automation and edge case identification
- Coordinates with Product Managers to clarify acceptance criteria
- Partners with Release Managers on smoke test execution and rollback validation
- Collaborates with UX Designers to validate user experience against design specs
- Supports Data Analysts with quality metrics and test coverage data

---

## Specialized & Supporting Roles

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate the agile development process, remove team impediments, and foster continuous improvement. They coach teams on agile best practices and ensure delivery flow.

### Responsibilities
- Organize and moderate daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
- Work with the Project Manager to identify and resolve team impediments
- Coach team on agile best practices and milestones
- Monitor sprint health and escalate blockers
- Facilitate process improvements based on retrospective feedback

### Goals
- Remove impediments to team velocity and delivery
- Foster psychological safety and team collaboration
- Maintain healthy sprint cadence and predictable delivery

### Typical Communication
- Sprint ceremonies (standup, planning, review, retrospective)
- One-on-ones with team members on blockers
- Sprint health reports to Project Manager

### Interactions
- Coordinates closely with the Project Manager for execution cadence and overall project health
- Facilitates communication between Developers and other roles to maintain flow
- Works with all team members to identify and remove impediments
- Supports continuous improvement through retrospective action items
- Partners with Release Manager on deployment day readiness and team coordination

---

## User Experience (UX) Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers advocate for user-centric product design, ensuring usability and accessibility in deliverables. They translate requirements into intuitive solutions that delight customers.

### Responsibilities
- Translate requirements into wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Run usability tests and gather user feedback
- Collaborate with Product Manager on feature prioritization and design validation
- Ensure accessibility and design consistency across features
- Create design system documentation

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support costs
- Validate design decisions through user research

### Typical Communication
- Design specs and prototypes in collaboration tools
- Usability test findings and recommendations
- Design review sessions with stakeholders

### Interactions
- Partners with Developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation quality
- Collaborates with Product Managers on feature scope and prioritization
- Works with QA to validate user experience against design specs
- Coordinates with Customer Success Leads to understand user pain points and satisfaction
- Supports Data Analysts with design-specific metrics and tracking

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts provide data-driven insights to inform project decisions and measure outcomes. They track key metrics and analyze trends to guide product and project decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Define and monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and success metrics
- Analyze user and project data for trends, patterns, and insights
- Support Product Manager in validating hypotheses and feature impact
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholder visibility
- Identify data quality issues and recommend improvements

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making across the project
- Measure and communicate project impact and ROI
- Identify opportunities for optimization and improvement

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews and dashboards
- Analysis reports and insights memos
- Stakeholder presentations on project outcomes

### Interactions
- Works with Product and Project Managers to set KPIs and understand project goals
- Collaborates with Developers on instrumentation and data collection requirements
- Partners with Customer Success Leads to track adoption metrics and customer satisfaction
- Supports Release Manager with pre/post-deployment analysis
- Works with QA on quality metrics and test coverage visibility

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers oversee the technical release process to production, coordinating release planning and risk mitigation. They ensure smooth, safe deployments with minimal customer impact.

### Responsibilities
- Plan release timelines and ensure readiness criteria are met
- Coordinate stakeholder communications during release windows
- Document release and rollback procedures
- Execute deployments and post-deploy verification
- Lead incident response for deployment-related issues

### Goals
- Ensure safe, predictable releases to production
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Maintain clear communication with all stakeholders during releases

### Typical Communication
- Release notes and deployment checklists
- Deployment window notifications to stakeholders
- Post-release status updates and incident reports

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Developers on deployment readiness and technical validation
- Works with QA to execute smoke tests and validate release quality
- Coordinates with Project Managers on release timing and stakeholder communication
- Partners with Data Analysts on pre/post-deployment metrics
- Supports incident response with Developers and on-call teams
- Coordinates with Scrum Master on team availability during deployment windows

---

## Customer Success Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Success Leads ensure successful post-release customer adoption and ongoing satisfaction. They act as the voice of the customer, feeding insights back to the product and project teams.

### Responsibilities
- Gather customer feedback and insights post-launch
- Liaise between customers and product/project teams for enhancements
- Lead customer onboarding and training for new features
- Monitor customer satisfaction and identify at-risk accounts
- Support feature adoption metrics and success measurement

### Goals
- Maximize customer adoption and satisfaction with new features
- Identify and escalate critical customer issues
- Provide customer-centric feedback to product and project teams

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and insights
- Feature adoption metrics and customer satisfaction reports
- Customer training materials and release communications

### Interactions
- Communicates key customer insights to Product and Project Managers for iteration planning
- Supports Developers with customer-reported issues and use case validation
- Collaborates with Data Analysts to track adoption metrics and customer satisfaction scores
- Works with UX Designers to incorporate customer feedback into future design improvements
- Partners with Release Manager on customer communication and feature launch coordination

---

## Stakeholders & Sponsors

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors provide business context, governance, and approvals. They represent business needs, customer perspectives, and organizational priorities.

### Responsibilities
- Provide business context and strategic alignment
- Approve key decisions and gate transitions
- Escalate blockers and resource constraints
- Communicate project status to broader organization

### Goals
- Ensure project aligns with business strategy
- Provide oversight and governance
- Remove organizational barriers to delivery

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates
- Decision approvals and gate reviews
- Escalation and issue resolution

### Interactions
- Receives project status from Project Manager
- Approves strategic decisions with Product Manager
- Escalates organizational blockers
- Provides feedback and guidance on business priorities
